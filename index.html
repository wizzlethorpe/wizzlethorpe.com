Dr. Bixby Wizzlethorpe

Overview

Dr. Bixby Wizzlethorpe is a renowned alchemist in Southaven, celebrated for his groundbreaking work in magical alchemical mixology and his eccentric personality. Brilliance unquestionable; methodology occasionally explosive.

Description

A wiry man in his late sixties, frizzy white hair defying gravity, thick-rimmed glasses magnifying bright inquisitive eyes. His robes: patchwork, scorched, ink-stained, pouch-laden. Voice: high, fast, trailing into tangential mutters. Despite the chaos, he radiates charm and curiosity.

Personality

Eccentric optimist; relentless learner. Enthusiasm eclipses practicality, spawning cluttered experiments and comedic mishaps. Kind-hearted, occasionally absent-minded, fiercely loyal and ready to risk much to help friends.

Background

Born to scholars; prodigious in arcane and alchemical arts. Traveled widely, apprenticing under masters and harvesting rare ingredients. Famous for groundbreaking magical cocktails. Settled in Southaven to found his laboratory.

Reputation tinged by incidents – such as transmuting half the town's water into a potion of minor invisibility. Expelled from the Marble Academy in Lusteris after accidentally summoning a Kraken. Wandered forests, reached Balandel, joined the Guild of the Wandering Flame, embracing alchemy and mixology.

In Session 1 - The Disappearances in Southaven, he gave adventurers a list of rare alchemical ingredients (Bixby's Cocktails). Bandit stash confusion revealed his emblem; Mira Fallbrook, his assistant, may have sold supplies without his knowledge.
